Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is not just the most famous building of the 20th century, but Sydney's home of opera. Climb the Monumental Steps as the sun sets over the harbor before enjoying a glass of champagne in the Northern Foyer of the Joan Sutherland Theatre, with its spectacular views of Sydney Harbour.

Location: Bennelong Point, Sydney, NSW 2000
Main Spaces: Concert Hall, Joan Sutherland Theatre, Drama Theatre, Playhouse, Studio
Capacity: Concert Hall: 2,679 | Joan Sutherland Theatre: 1,507
Contact: 1300 764 849
